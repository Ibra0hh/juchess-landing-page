Our Vision

To make chess part of university life.

We believe every student should have a board to sit at and a community to play with. Our mission is simple — grow the game on campus, and help everyone who plays it get better.

01
Spread the game

Bring chess to more University of Jordan students — open meetups, beginner-friendly nights, and a club anyone can walk into, whatever their rating.

02
Play in person

Run real over-the-board tournaments on campus — the handshake, the clock, the tension across the table that online play can never replace.

03
Sharpen skills

Help every member improve — weekly training, game reviews, and friendly competition that turns casual players into confident competitors.
